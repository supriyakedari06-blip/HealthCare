What is Ayu Sutra?

We are a digital health platform designed to simplify medical management. From identifying unknown pills to digitizing your medical ID, we use cutting-edge AI to keep you safe and informed.

Our "Mind Gym" and "Voice Prescription" tools are built to reduce cognitive load for both patients and healthcare providers.

🔒 Privacy First

Your health data is encrypted and never shared. We follow strict international standards to ensure your Health ID remains yours alone.
